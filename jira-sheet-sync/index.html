Sheet Sync for Jira

Purpose: this Google Sheets™ add-on, published by Cody Labs, connects to your Jira Cloud site with an API token you provide and imports issues matching any JQL query into your own Google Sheet — on demand or on an automatic schedule — so teams can build reports and dashboards without exporting CSVs by hand.

It accesses only the spreadsheet it runs in. Issue data flows directly from your Jira site to your sheet; we operate no servers and never receive your issues or credentials.
