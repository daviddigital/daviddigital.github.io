Excel Upsert Importer for monday.com

Purpose: this monday.com board view, published by Cody Labs, lets you upload an Excel or CSV file to a monday board and update-or-insert items matched on a key column — so repeated imports update existing items instead of creating duplicate rows.

It runs entirely in your browser inside monday.com, using your own monday session. Your spreadsheet is processed locally; we operate no servers and your data never leaves your monday account.
