Zendesk Exporter for Google Sheets

Purpose: this Google Sheets™ add-on, published by Cody Labs, connects to your Zendesk account with an API token you provide and exports your support tickets into your own Google Sheet — on demand or on an automatic hourly, daily, or weekly schedule.

It accesses only the spreadsheet it runs in. Ticket data flows directly from your Zendesk to your sheet; we operate no servers and never receive your data.
