Bulk Importer for Xero

Purpose: this Google Sheets™ add-on, published by Cody Labs, validates invoices and contacts prepared in your spreadsheet and pushes them into your Xero organisation — with a full pre-flight validation report before anything is written, so bad rows are caught before they reach your books.

It accesses only the spreadsheet it runs in and connects to Xero through Xero’s official OAuth flow. Data flows directly from your sheet to your Xero organisation; we operate no servers and never receive your financial data.
